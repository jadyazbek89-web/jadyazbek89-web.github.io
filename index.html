Flagship Case Study

Visit Qatar

Tourism Account Director Meta · TikTok · Snapchat

Qatar Tourism needed to position the country as a premier travel destination across GCC and international markets. The challenge: build sustained digital presence that felt native, not broadcast, across three platforms simultaneously.

As Account Director, I led the digital strategy across channel planning, content architecture, social media strategy, and creator coordination. From PSG and UFC activations to LEGO, ITTF, and Qatar Food Festival moments, every campaign decision connected back to one goal: drive Qatar into the travel consideration set.

The Challenge

Break into the GCC leisure travel market against established competitors, managing bilingual content (Arabic + English) across families, sports fans, and culture seekers, on three platforms with different audiences and formats. Beyond reach, the brief demanded credibility: content that felt native to Qatar rather than produced for it, at a time when the destination was still building its global recognition.

The Strategy

Audience-first content pillars: Culture, sports, food, adventure, each mapped to platform and segment. Platform-native formats: reels for TikTok, carousels for Meta, influencer-led for Snapchat. Event-driven amplification: PSG, FIFA, Qatar Food Festival, real moments turned into always-on reach. Creator selection prioritized regional authenticity over follower count, and an editorial calendar ensured consistent publishing cadence while keeping room for real-time campaign moments.

The Execution

Coordinated content across PSG athletes, local creators, and Qatar Tourism's internal teams. Developed social media collabs and activation strategy for UFC Fight Night in Qatar, working with the UFC team who reported directly to Dana White, alongside Qatar Tourism stakeholders. Managed content calendars and publishing strategy across platforms, turning high-profile events into consistent, on-brand social moments. Bilingual campaigns, localized targeting, cross-platform brand consistency at scale.
