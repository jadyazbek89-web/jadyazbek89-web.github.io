How I Think

The Approach

01

Audience before platform

I don't decide where to show up until I know exactly who I'm talking to. Channel selection without audience clarity is just noise with a budget behind it.

02

Data informs. Instinct decides.

Numbers tell you what happened. Strategy is what you do next. The strongest campaigns live in the space between rigorous analytics and earned judgment.

03

Built to scale, not just to ship.

A campaign that can't compound isn't a campaign; it's an event. I build systems with architecture that grows past the brief and survives the next budget cycle.
